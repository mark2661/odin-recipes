Jam Sandwich Recipe

Description

A quick easy delicious recipe for people on the go!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • 1 Tin of baked beans
  • 40g Chedder

Steps

  1. Toast bread
  2. Warm beans on hob for 3-4 mins. Stir constantly
  3. Grate cheese
  4. Place toast on plate and pour on beans
  5. Sprinkle with cheese
